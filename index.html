What is Mark Six?

Mark Six is a popular lottery game in Hong Kong, organized by the Hong Kong Jockey Club. Players choose 6 numbers from 1 to 49, and a draw is held three times a week to determine the winning combination.


How It Works

Six main numbers and one extra number are drawn. The more matching numbers you have, the higher the prize tier.
